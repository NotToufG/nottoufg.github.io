Last Breath Simulator

Set "earbreak" to 50 if you wish to use both of your speakers, set it to 0 for only left and 100 for right.

Controls:

Press space to change soul type

Press z for stay-in-middle blasters

Press x for cross blasters

Press c for giant blasters

Press 1 for random direction blasters

Press s for bone stab

Press h for bones

Press e for spinning bones

Press i or j for spinning blasters

Press , for explosive bones

Press w after pressing space for sans 2nd attack

Press 2 to end battle with impossible attack

(while playing, click on the page to start playing audio)
